Today, June 9, 2025, McKesson Corporation's (McKesson) executives are scheduled to meet with various investors and analysts. During these meetings, they will discuss McKesson’s growth strategies, operational performance, business and market trends and financial outlook. Today, McKesson is providing the following updates to its Fiscal Year 2026 Financial Outlook. McKesson is increasing its full year Adjusted Earnings per Diluted Share guide from a range of $36.75 to $37.55, to a new range of $36.90 to $37.70. McKesson is reaffirming its full year effective tax rate guidance of 17% to 19%. McKesson anticipates the effective tax rate for the first half of the fiscal year to be 17% to 20%, with a higher rate in Q1 than Q2, and the second half effective tax rate to be 16% to 19%.
