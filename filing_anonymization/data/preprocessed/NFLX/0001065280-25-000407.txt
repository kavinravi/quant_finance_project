(Nasdaq: NFLX) announced today that its Board of Directors has approved a ten-for-one forward stock split of the Company’s common stock. The split will be effected through an amendment to the Company’s Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation. The purpose of the stock split is to reset the market price of the Company’s common stock to a range that will be more accessible to employees who participate in the Company’s stock option program.
