On June 6, 2017, StreamVault Media (the “Company”) held its annual meeting of stockholders in Maplewood, Washington (“Annual Meeting”). As of April 10, 2017, the Company’s record date, there were a total of 431,004,439 shares of common stock outstanding and entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting. At the Annual Meeting, 387,103,194 shares of common stock were represented in person or by proxy and, therefore, a quorum was present. The stockholders of the Company voted on the following items at the Annual Meeting: 1. The election of three Class III directors to hold office until the 2020 Annual Meeting of Stockholders; 2. The ratification of the appointment of Ernst & [COMPANY] as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for the year ending December 31, 2017; 3. Advisory approval of the Company’s executive officer compensation; 4. Advisory approval of the frequency of votes on the Company’s executive officer compensation; 5. A stockholder proposal regarding proxy access; 6. A stockholder proposal regarding an annual sustainability report; 7. A stockholder proposal regarding an emissions report; 8. A stockholder proposal regarding electing each director annually; 9. A stockholder proposal regarding simple majority vote; and 10. A stockholder proposal to amend Section 3.3 of the bylaws to majority vote. For more information about the foregoing proposals, see the Company’s definitive proxy statement dated April 24, 2017.
