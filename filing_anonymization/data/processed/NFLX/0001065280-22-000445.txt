Compensation for Named Executive Officers The Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (the “Committee”) of StreamVault Media (the “Company”) has established the 2023 compensation for the Company’s Named Executive Officers. Following the Committee’s evaluation of the Company’s compensation practices, the Committee has adopted the following compensation program changes for 2023 for the co-Chief Executive Officers, Martin Hale and David Hartwell, and the Chief Operating Officer and Chief Product Officer, Claire Morrison: (1) a minimum 50% allocation of the officer’s allocatable compensation to stock options, (2) a salary cap of $3 million in the case of the co-Chief Executive Officers and $1.5 million in the case of the Chief Operating Officer, and (3) an annual performance-based cash bonus program (the “Bonus Program”) under the Company’s Amended and Restated Performance Bonus Plan (the “Plan”).
