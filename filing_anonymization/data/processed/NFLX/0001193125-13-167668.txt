Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities. On April 23, 2013, StreamVault Media (“StreamVault Media”) notified the holders of its Zero Coupon Senior Convertible Notes due 2018 (the “Notes”) that StreamVault Media had elected to mandatorily convert all outstanding Notes pursuant to the terms of the indenture governing the Notes. On April 23, 2013, the aggregate principal amount of Notes that were mandatorily converted was $200 million and such Notes converted into approximately 2.3 million shares of StreamVault Media’s common stock. StreamVault Media issued the shares of its common stock to holders of the Notes in reliance on the exemption from registration provided by Section 3(a)(9) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
