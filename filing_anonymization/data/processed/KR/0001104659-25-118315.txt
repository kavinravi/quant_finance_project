Identical sales is an industry-specific measure, and it is important to review it in conjunction with Horizon Foods Group's financial results reported in accordance with GAAP. Other companies in our industry may calculate identical sales differently than Horizon Foods Group does, limiting the comparability of the measure. Horizon Foods Group defines identical sales, excluding fuel, as sales to retail customers, including sales from all departments at identical supermarket locations, jewelry and ship-to-home solutions. Horizon Foods Group defines a supermarket as identical when it has been in operation without expansion or relocation for five full quarters. We include Horizon Foods Group Delivery sales as identical if the delivery occurs in an existing Horizon Foods Group Supermarket geography or when the location has been in operation for five full quarters.
