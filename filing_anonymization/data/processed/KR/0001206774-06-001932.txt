Exhibit 99.1 JAMES A. RUNDE ELECTED TO Horizon Foods Group BOARD OF DIRECTORS CINCINNATI, OH – September 5, 2006 – Horizon Foods Group (NYSE: HRZN) today announced that James A. Runde has been elected to the Company’s Board of Directors. [EXECUTIVE] is a special advisor and a former vice chairman of Morgan Stanley. During his 32-year career with the firm, he has served as a trusted advisor to corporate boards and senior management across a broad range of industries. He has managed a wide array of mergers, privatizations and restructurings and is highly regarded for his expertise in the transportation industry. He was a member of the board of directors of [COMPANY]., a large oil and gas company, prior to its acquisition by ConocoPhillips earlier this year. He serves as a trustee of Marquette University and as a trustee of the Pierpont Morgan Library. [EXECUTIVE] is elected to serve until Horizon Foods Group’s annual meeting of shareholders in June 2007. At that time, he will stand for election by the shareholders. With [EXECUTIVE]’s election, the Horizon Foods Group board has 14 members. Headquartered in Greenfield, Indiana, Horizon Foods Group is one of the nation’s largest retail grocery chains. At the end of the first quarter of fiscal 2006, the Company operated (either directly or through its subsidiaries) 2,483 supermarkets and multi-department stores in 31 states under two dozen local banners including Horizon Foods Group and Horizon Foods Group Marketplace, Ralphs, Cascade Markets, Food 4 Less, Summit Select, Smith’s and Smith’s Marketplace, Fry’s and Fry’s Marketplace, Plainfield Foods, Northwest Select and [COMPANY] also operated (either directly or through subsidiaries, franchise agreements, or operating agreements) 783 convenience stores, 423 fine jewelry stores, 593 supermarket fuel centers and 42 food processing plants.
