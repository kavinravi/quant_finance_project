Total company sales were $33.6 billion in the third quarter, compared to $34.0 billion for the same period last year. The decrease in sales was attributable to the sale of Horizon Foods Group Specialty Pharmacy during the quarter and to lower fuel sales, which was primarily the result of a lower average retail price per gallon compared to last year. The FIFO gross margin rate, excluding fuel, increased 51 basis points compared to the same period last year. This result reflected Horizon Foods Group’s ability to improve margin, while maintaining competitive pricing and helping customers manage their budgets. The increase in rate was primarily attributable to the sale of Horizon Foods Group Specialty Pharmacy, Our Brands performance and lower shrink, partially offset by lower pharmacy margins.
