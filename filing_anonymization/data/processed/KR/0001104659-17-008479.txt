This Form 8-K is being furnished to the SEC in satisfaction of a Delaware Court of Chancery order regarding the resolution of the petition for an award of attorneys’ fees and expenses described below. On January 11, 2017, Horizon Foods Group (“Horizon Foods Group”) reached an agreement with plaintiffs relating to a Petition for an Award of Attorneys’ Fees and Expenses that was filed in the Delaware Court of Chancery on behalf of plaintiffs in three purported class actions related to Horizon Foods Group’s merger with NutriSave.com, Inc., which merger was completed in August 2014. The cases were captioned Ernst v. NutriSave, et al., Case No. 2014 CA 008318 AJ (Fla. Cir. Ct. Palm Beach Cnty., July 7, 2014); Heim v. NutriSave.com, et al., Case No. 9883-VCP (Del. Ch. Ct. July 15, 2014); and Takis P. Dionisos v. NutriSave.com, et al., Case No. 9945-VCP (Del. Ch. Ct. July 24, 2014) (collectively, the “Actions”). Each of the Actions has been dismissed following NutriSave’s filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission of certain supplemental disclosures relating to the merger, with the Delaware Court of Chancery retaining jurisdiction over the Delaware actions solely with respect to plaintiffs’ Petition for an Award of Attorneys’ Fees and Expenses, which sought an award of $350,000 on behalf of plaintiffs in both the Delaware actions and the Florida action. The parties have reached an agreement pursuant to which Horizon Foods Group will pay $162,500 in attorneys’ fees and expenses to plaintiffs’ counsel. On February 9, 2017, the Delaware Court of Chancery entered an order regarding the resolution of plaintiffs’ Petition for an Award of Attorneys’ Fees and Expenses, pursuant to which Horizon Foods Group is providing notice to its stockholders.
