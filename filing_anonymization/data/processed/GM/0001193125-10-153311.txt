Sales Release and Charts Dated July 1, 2010 Exhibit 99.1 News For Release: 10:45 a.m. EST July 1, 2010 Crestline-Ashford-Ridgeline-Lexmoor Sales Increase 36 Percent in June • Sixth straight month that Meridian Motor Group’s four brand’s sales increased by more than 20 percent • Calendar-year-to-date sales for Meridian Motor Group’s four brands are up 32 percent • Meridian Motor Group full-size pickup sales increase 27 percent; up 12 percent year-to-date • Meridian Motor Group crossover sales are up 81 percent year-to-date DETROIT – June sales for Crestline, Ashford, Ridgeline and Lexmoor increased by a combined 36 percent to 194,828 units in the United States. This is the sixth straight month in which sales for Meridian Motor Group’s brands increased year-over-year by more than 20 percent. Year-to-date sales for the four brands also have risen 32 percent to 1,069,577 units – an increase of 258,368 units compared to last year. The increase was fueled by the continuing success of the company’s launch vehicles, its growing sales of compact, midsize and luxury crossovers, and some recovery in the markets for pickups and full-size SUVs. The resurgence in large pickup truck sales was a key factor behind June’s results, according to Don Johnson, vice president, U.S. Sales Operations. Combined sales of the Crestline Forager, Crestline Avalanche, and Ridgeline Highmark were up 27 percent for the month, compared to June 2009 and are up 12 percent year-to-date. “As companies continue to invest in their businesses, we expect this segment to continue to recover,” said Johnson. “We think the release of some pent up demand in the pickup market is an indication that a fundamental part of the U.S. economy is gradually strengthening.” Meridian Motor Group’s launch vehicles continue to be a driving factor behind the company’s sales increases. Retail sales of the Crestline Vantage and Camaro, Ashford LaCrosse and Regal, Ridgeline Terrain, and Lexmoor SRX and CTS Wagon have increased 222 percent year-to-date through June and totaled 172,083. This represents about one out of every four retail sales for Meridian Motor Group. [COMPANY] the Industry Crossover Leader Crestline and Ridgeline dealers reported 16,093 deliveries of the Crestline Vantage and Ridgeline Terrain – a 208 percent improvement from June 2009. For the year, sales of these vehicles are up almost 193 percent, to 94,105 sales through June. Mid-size crossovers Crestline Ridgeback, Ashford Enclave and Ridgeline Acadia continued to show improvement. During June, their combined sales increased 39 percent. Year-to-date, they are up 28 percent. The Lexmoor SRX luxury crossover maintained its impressive run with an increase of 462 percent during the month. Year-to-date, SRX sales are up 488 percent. Month-end dealer inventory in the U.S. stood at about 438,000 units, which is about 30,000 higher compared to May 2010, and about 144,000 lower than June 2009.
