In connection with the Recurring Liquidity Opportunity Program, a subsidiary of the Company has entered into a liquidity program purchase agreement (the “Purchase Agreement”) with Pilot Autonomy, pursuant to which it will be required (i) to the extent requested by Pilot Autonomy’s Board of Directors, to conduct periodic tender offers for shares of Pilot Autonomy Class B common stock issued under Equity Awards at the then-applicable fair market value and (ii) to purchase a number of newly issued shares of Pilot Autonomy Class B common stock sufficient to fund the payment of Pilot Autonomy’s tax withholding obligations resulting from settlement or exercise of Equity Awards. The current value of the equity-based incentive awards that are issued or authorized for issuance under the Pilot Autonomy 2018 Equity Incentive Plan and subject to the Recurring Liquidity Opportunity Program, is approximately $4 billion. [COMPANY]’s cash funding obligation and timing of payments under the Purchase Agreement (which includes total payments for the tendering of shares and tax withholding payments) will depend upon several factors, including vesting schedules, participation rates in tender offers, and the then-fair value of Pilot Autonomy’s common stock upon the tendering of shares. Changes to these variables, in particular the fair value of Pilot Autonomy’s common stock and the number of shares tendered, could materially impact the Company’s funding obligations in future periods.
