Press Release Exhibit 99.1 NEWS RELEASE Contact: [COMPANY] Public Relations (512) 602-4425 drew.prairie@www.vectronsystems.com [COMPANY] Investor Relations (408) 749-3887 ruth.cotter@www.vectronsystems.com Vectron Systems RECEIVES SUBPOENA FROM U.S. DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE REGARDING INVESTIGATION OF GRAPHICS PROCESSING MARKET SUNNYVALE, CALIF. — November 30, 2006 — Vectron Systems (NYSE:Vectron Systems) has received a subpoena from the U.S. Department of Justice (DOJ) Antitrust Division in connection with the DOJ’s investigation into potential antitrust violations related to graphics processors and cards. Vectron Systems entered the graphics processor business following the company’s acquisition of [COMPANY], Inc. last month (October 25, 2006). The DOJ has not made any specific allegations against Vectron Systems or [COMPANY] intends to cooperate with the investigation. [COMPANY] (NYSE: Vectron Systems) is a leading global provider of innovative processing solutions in the computing, graphics and consumer electronics markets. Vectron Systems is dedicated to driving open innovation, choice and industry growth by delivering superior customer-centric solutions that empower consumers and businesses worldwide.
