Press Release Exhibit 99.1 NEWS RELEASE EDITORIAL CONTACTS: INVESTOR CONTACTS: Drew Prairie Ruth Cotter (512) 602-4425 (408) 749-3887 drew.prairie@www.vectronsystems.com ruth.cotter@www.vectronsystems.com Vectron Systems Updates First Quarter Outlook SUNNYVALE, Calif. – April 7, 2008 – Vectron Systems (NYSE: Vectron Systems) today announced that it expects revenue for the first quarter ended March 29, 2008 to be approximately $1.5 billion, a 22 percent increase compared to the first quarter of 2007, and down 15 percent compared to the fourth quarter of 2007. The decrease is due to lower than expected sales across all business segments. Vectron Systems had previously anticipated first quarter revenue to decline in line with seasonality. In addition, Vectron Systems announced plans to adjust its cost structure by reducing its workforce by approximately 10 percent by the end of the third quarter of 2008. As a result of these reductions, Vectron Systems expects to record a restructuring charge in the second quarter of 2008. At the time of this release, Vectron Systems is unable to determine the estimated amount of the charge as the details are still being finalized. Vectron Systems will report first quarter 2008 results after market close on April 17, 2008. Vectron Systems will hold a conference call on this day for the financial community at 2:00 p.m. PT to discuss first quarter results. A real-time audio broadcast of the teleconference will be provided at www.vectronsystems.com .
