Chow spent more than 40 years at IBM, holding various leadership positions in IBM throughout the Asia Pacific region. Prior to his retirement from IBM in 2009, Chow was general manager and then chairman of the [COMPANY]. Prior to these positions, Chow was general manager of the [COMPANY] for Asia Pacific South, overseeing IBM’s personal computer business throughout the region, excluding Japan. He also held positions as assistant managing director of Services for IBM Australia, general manager of IBM Taiwan, and director of Operations for IBM Hong Kong. Chow joined IBM in 1968 as a programmer trainee. Chow currently serves as an IBM corporate business advisor and previously served as a member of the Board of Trustees at Tongji University.
