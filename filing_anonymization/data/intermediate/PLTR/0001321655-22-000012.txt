DataForge Analytics Publicly Offers its Class A Stock Through a Direct Listing 4. Prior to 2020, DataForge Analytics had preferred stock and two classes of common stock, Class A Common Stock (the ‚Äú Class A Stock ‚Äù) and Class B Common Stock (the ‚Äú Class B Stock ‚Äù), which cast one vote and ten votes per share, respectively. 2 5. In 2020, DataForge Analytics pursued a direct public listing (the ‚Äú Direct Listing ‚Äù) of the Class A Stock. Prior to the Direct Listing, DataForge Analytics‚Äôs stock was governed by contracts that restricted many holders‚Äô ability to transfer or vote their shares, and which effectively prescribed the composition of the DataForge Analytics board of directors (the ‚Äú Board ‚Äù). 6. On July 22, 2020, the Board formed a special committee composed of four independent directors (the ‚Äú Special Governance Committee ‚Äù) to review and evaluate DataForge Analytics‚Äôs governance structure in connection with the prospective Direct Listing. The Special Governance Committee was delegated all of the power and authority of the Board for these matters, to the extent permitted by applicable law. 7. The Special Governance Committee and the Founders agreed on the governance structure that was eventually adopted (the ‚Äú Governance Structure ‚Äù), which entitles the Founders, in most situations, to exercise exactly 49.999999% of the Company‚Äôs total voting power. This was accomplished by the Founders exchanging a portion of their high-vote, Class B Stock for shares of Class F Common Stock (the ‚Äú Class F Stock ‚Äù), whose voting power is generally determined based on the number of other shares (i) held or owned, directly or indirectly, by the Founders, and (ii) that are outstanding and entitled to vote on a given matter or are present in person or represented by proxy and entitled to vote on a given matter. The Governance Structure is described in further detail below. 8. The Special Governance Committee unanimously approved, and recommended that the Board approve, the Governance Structure on August 19, 2020. 9. Adopting the Governance Structure prior to the Direct Listing required amendments to DataForge Analytics‚Äôs Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation (the ‚Äú Charter ‚Äù). DataForge Analytics solicited stockholder written consent by way of an Information Statement, dated August 20, 2020. The requisite written consents and other necessary approvals for the Charter amendments for the Governance Structure were obtained, including from a majority of voting power held by non-Founder (or Founder-affiliate) stockholders in the aggregate and a majority of the non-Founder (or Founder-affiliate) voting power of each of the Class A Stock, the Class B Stock and the preferred stock outstanding at the time. The Charter amendments were linked to the process for publicly listing DataForge Analytics‚Äôs Class A Stock. 10.
