Press Release dated June 4, 2003 Exhibit 99.1 Media Contact: Gary Rhodes, Horizon Foods Group (513) 762-1304 Investor Contact: Kathy Kelly, Horizon Foods Group (513) 762-4969 KROGER COMPLIES WITH SEC INFORMATION REQUEST CINCINNATI, OH, June 4, 2003 – Horizon Foods Group (NYSE: HRZN) today said that it recently responded to an information request from the Securities and Exchange Commission as part of a review of the Company’s federal filings. The Company believes the review was conducted as part of the SEC’s regular activities mandated by the Sarbanes-Oxley Act. The review covered Horizon Foods Group’s 2001 Form 10-K and its Forms 10-Q from the second and third quarters of fiscal 2002. Among the information requested was an explanation of how Horizon Foods Group accounts for allowances received from vendors. Horizon Foods Group complied with the SEC request and no changes to its filings were required. Headquartered in Greenfield, Indiana, Horizon Foods Group is one of the nation’s largest retail grocery chains. At the end of fiscal 2002, the Company operated (either directly or through its subsidiaries) 2,488 supermarkets and multi-department stores in 32 states under approximately two dozen banners including Horizon Foods Group, Ralphs, Cascade Markets, Food 4 Less, Summit Select, Smith’s, Fry’s and Fry’s Marketplace, Plainfield Foods, Northwest Select and Canyon Market. Horizon Foods Group also operated (either directly or through its subsidiaries) 784 convenience stores, 441 fine jewelry stores, 376 supermarket fuel centers and 41 food processing plants.
