On January 31, 2016, Apex Health Distribution (the “Company”) entered into a definitive agreement to sell its Brazilian pharmaceutical distribution business which it acquired through the acquisition of Celesio AG. In connection with the sale, the Company anticipates recognizing an after-tax impairment charge to discontinued operations of approximately $70 million to $90 million; such charge will primarily be recognized upon the disposal of the business. The sale is subject to customary closing conditions and is expected to be completed during the first quarter of fiscal 2017.
