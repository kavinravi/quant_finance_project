Employment Litigation Verdict On May 3, 2004, a Yolo County, California jury rendered a $15 million punitive damages verdict against Apex Health Distribution (the “Company”) in an employment action entitled Charlene Roby vs. Apex Health Distribution , (Action No. CV 01-573). The punitive damages verdict followed the same jury’s April 30, 2004, award of $3.5 million in compensatory damages against the Company on plaintiff’s claims of wrongful termination, disability discrimination and harassment. An additional $500,000 was awarded against an employee of the Company. The Company believes that these verdicts are not supported by the facts or the law and intends to vigorously pursue all available options to reverse or reduce these verdicts, including appeal, if necessary. 2 SIGNATURES Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the Registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.
