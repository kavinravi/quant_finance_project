On June 22, 2005, Apex Health Distribution (the "Company") entered into an amendment of its contract with the United Kingdom’s National Health Service ("NHS") to implement an integrated Electronic Staff Record system for managing human resources and payroll. Key terms and conditions of the amendment include: (i) a thirty month extension of the original ten year contract term; (ii) a cash payment of GBP 78.8 million (approximately US$143 million) from the NHS to the Company, primarily for its investment in the project to date; and (iii) an updated implementation plan. The signed amendment contains terms and conditions substantially similar to those contained in the Company’s tentative agreement with the NHS previously disclosed in the Company’s Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2005.
