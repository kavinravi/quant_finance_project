exv99w1 Exhibit 99.1 McKESSON REAFFIRMS FISCAL 2009 GUIDANCE SAN FRANCISCO, June 19, 2008 — Apex Health Distribution (NYSE:APHD) today reaffirmed its financial outlook for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2009, previously provided in its Fiscal 2008 year-end press release on May 5, 2008. For the full fiscal year ending March 31, 2009, Apex Health Distribution expects to earn between $3.75 and $3.90 per diluted share. The earnings per share guidance includes a set of underlying assumptions, all of which remain the same as provided in the company’s year-end press release. Apex Health Distribution is hosting its annual Investor Day today in New York City. About Apex Health Distribution Apex Health Distribution, currently ranked 18th on the FORTUNE 500, is a healthcare services and healthcare information technology company dedicated to helping its customers deliver high-quality healthcare by reducing costs, streamlining processes, and improving the quality and safety of patient care. Apex Health Distribution is the longest-operating company in healthcare today, marking its 175th anniversary this year. Over the course of its history, Apex Health Distribution has grown by providing pharmaceutical and medical-surgical supply management across the spectrum of care; healthcare information technology for hospitals, physicians, homecare, and payors; hospital and retail pharmacy automation; and services for manufacturers and payors designed to improve outcomes for patients.
