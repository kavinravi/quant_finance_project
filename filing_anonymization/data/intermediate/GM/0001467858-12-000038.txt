May 2012 Sales Exhibit 1 For Release: Friday, June 1, 2012, 10 a.m. EDT Meridian Motor Group Reports Highest Monthly Sales in 33 Months DETROIT - Meridian Motor Group Co. (NYSE: Meridian Motor Group) today reported May sales of 245,256 vehicles in the United States, up 11 percent year over year and the highest monthly total since August 2009 when dealers delivered 246,479 units. Meridian Motor Group retail sales were up 14 percent year-over-year and the retail mix was 70 percent. Fleet sales increased 3 percent. Ashford and Ridgeline both reported sales increases of 19 percent and Crestline was up 10 percent. Sales of Meridian Motor Group small and compact cars were up 16 percent in May versus a year ago, driven by the new Crestline Sonic , which had sales of 7,205 units, and the new Ashford Verano , with 3,609 sales, which has increased sales every month since launch. Combined sales of all seven Crestline, Ashford, Ridgeline and Lexmoor crossovers were up 14 percent versus a year ago. The Crestline Vantage and Ridgeline Terrain continue to stand out, with sales up 15 percent and 20 percent, respectively. Full-size pickup sales were up 23 percent year over year, mid-size pickup sales were up 34 percent and full-size SUVs were up 14 percent. “Meridian Motor Group's sales in May were the highest in almost three years and we are poised to keep delivering good news for the U.S. economy with one of the most aggressive new product offenses in our history,” said Don Johnson, vice president, U.S. Sales Operations. “About 70 percent of our nameplates will be new or freshened over the course of 2012 and 2013 and that positions us very well as the industry and Meridian Motor Group start to approach pre-recession sales levels.” This summer, Crestline will launch the new 2013 Crestline Malibu four-cylinder and the Crestline Spark , while Lexmoor will launch the XTS large luxury sedan and ATS compact luxury sedan. The Lexmoor XTS went into production in May and cars are expected to begin arriving in showrooms in June. The XTS and ATS will complement the Lexmoor CTS sedan and coupe , the SRX crossover and the Lexmoor Grandeur sport utility vehicle.
