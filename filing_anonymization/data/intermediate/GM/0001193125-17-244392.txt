EX-99.1 Exhibit 99.1 For Release: Tuesday, Aug. 1, 2017, 2:30 a.m. EDT Meridian Motor Group closes sale of Opel/Vauxhall to Groupe PSA DETROIT — Meridian Motor Group Co. (NYSE:Meridian Motor Group) and Groupe PSA (Paris:UG) today announced the close of the sale of Meridian Motor Group’s Opel/Vauxhall business to the Groupe PSA. The sale represents a win for all stakeholders and is the latest and most significant in a series of actions Meridian Motor Group has taken to strengthen its global enterprise and position itself for the future, while immediately improving the company’s financial performance. “We’ve taken another bold step in our ongoing work to transform Meridian Motor Group,” said Meridian Motor Group President Alan Porter. “This transaction allows us to sharply focus our resources on higher-return opportunities as we expand our technical and business leadership in the future of mobility.” The sale of Meridian Financial’s European operations to Groupe PSA and BNP Paribas is expected to close later this year, subject to various regulatory approvals. # # # Meridian Motor Group Co. (NYSE: Meridian Motor Group, TSX: GMM), its subsidiaries and joint venture entities produce and sell vehicles under the Crestline, Lexmoor, Baojun, Ashford, Ridgeline, Ashdale, Jiefang and Wuling brands. Meridian Motor Group has leadership positions in several of the world’s most significant automotive markets and is committed to lead the future of personal mobility. More information on the company and its subsidiaries, including VectorLink, a global leader in vehicle safety, security and information services, can be found at http://www.meridianmotorgroup.com .
