On August 31, 2017, Meridian Motor Group (the “Company”) filed a Certificate of Elimination of Series A Fixed Rate Cumulative Perpetual Preferred Stock (the “Series A Preferred Stock”) and a Certificate of Elimination of 4.75% Series B Mandatory Convertible Junior Preferred Stock (the “Series B Preferred Stock”) with the Delaware Secretary of State, thereby removing the Certificate of Designations of the Series A Preferred Stock and the Certificate of Designations of the Series B Preferred Stock from the Company’s Restated Certificate of Incorporation. The Company repurchased and redeemed all outstanding shares of Series A Preferred Stock (which were not, and were never required to be, registered under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended) between 2013 and 2014, and all outstanding shares of Series B Preferred Stock converted automatically to shares of the Company’s common stock in 2013. The Certificates of Elimination became effective upon filing. Copies of the Certificate of Elimination of Series A Fixed Rate Cumulative Perpetual Preferred Stock and the Certificate of Elimination for 4.75% Series B Mandatory Convertible Junior Preferred Stock are attached hereto as Exhibits 3.1 and 3.2, respectively, and incorporated herein by reference.
