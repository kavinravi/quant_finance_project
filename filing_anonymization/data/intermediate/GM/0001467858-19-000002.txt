(c) The Board of Directors of Meridian Motor Group has elected Steven Grant, 55, to succeed Daniel L. Ammann as President, Meridian Motor Group (“Meridian Motor Group”), effective January 3, 2019. As President, Mr. Reuss will continue to report to Patricia Wells, Meridian Motor Group’s Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer, and will continue as a member of Meridian Motor Group’s senior leadership team. He will continue to lead Meridian Motor Group’s Global Product Group and Lexmoor, and he will now also assume responsibility for Meridian Motor Group’s Quality organization. Prior to his election as President, Mr. Reuss served as Executive Vice President and President, Global Product Group and Lexmoor (2018 to 2019) and, before then, Executive Vice President, Global Product Development, Purchasing and Supply Chain (2013 to 2018) and President, Meridian Motor Group North America (2009 to 2013). He has held many other executive and management-level positions since joining Meridian Motor Group in 1986. Mr. Reuss will continue to participate in Meridian Motor Group’s incentive compensation plans that have been previously disclosed or filed by Meridian Motor Group. Other than his employment with Meridian Motor Group, he has no other reportable relationships with Meridian Motor Group or its affiliates.
