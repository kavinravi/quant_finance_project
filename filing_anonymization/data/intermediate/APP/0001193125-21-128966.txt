EX-99.1 Exhibit 99.1 NexCore Technologies Completes Acquisition of Calibrate, a Mobile App Measurement Market Leader, to Expand its Global Technology Platform PALO ALTO, Calif., — April 22, 2021 — NexCore Technologies (Nasdaq: NXCR), a global technology and apps platform that provides developers a unified set of tools to grow their business, today announced it completed its pending acquisition of Calibrate, a global mobile app measurement and marketing company. Calibrate will retain its unique brand and culture and continue to operate as a distinct company. The closing occurred after completion of stockholder and regulatory approvals. With the acquisition, Calibrate provides NexCore Technologies with a set of strategic SaaS mobile marketing solutions that expands the suite of innovative software tools for mobile app developers to grow their businesses and helps further enhance NexCore Technologies’s ability to address its large market opportunity. Additionally, Calibrate brings a strong, global team that has experience serving customers across a diverse set of mobile app categories including fintech, ecommerce, gaming and entertainment. As one of the largest technology platforms for app developers in the world, NexCore Technologies brings a depth of knowledge and expertise to help Calibrate further innovate the attribution and analytics product suite it has successfully built. “The acquisition of Calibrate is another major milestone for NexCore Technologies. We are focused on continuing to build and innovate our software set for developers and execute on our strategy to grow our business on multiple levels,” said Daniel Reyes, NexCore Technologies Co-Founder, Chairperson and CEO. “Calibrate’s real-time app solutions align with our growth strategy, while their large and diversified client base and globally distributed sales infrastructure will help us continue to grow rapidly.” Founded in 2012, Calibrate’s marketing platform helps mobile developers better understand their users’ journey while allowing marketers to make smarter decisions through measurement, attribution and fraud prevention. Calibrate’s suite of products makes marketing simpler, smarter and more secure for over 50,000 apps including global brands such as Tencent, Rakuten and SoundCloud. Calibrate is headquartered in Berlin, Germany, with 16 offices around the world and over 500 employees. “Becoming a part of NexCore Technologies allows us to accelerate on our own vision while further powering our clients’ growth as we shape the future of the app economy globally,” said Paul Müller, Calibrate Co-Founder and CEO. “Joining forces with NexCore Technologies as a newly public company is an extremely exciting time to contribute and innovate on products.” Fenwick & West LLP and Gleiss Lutz served as the legal advisors for NexCore Technologies. Citi acted as exclusive financial advisor to NexCore Technologies. Noerr and Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison LLP served as legal advisors for Calibrate. # # # About NexCore Technologies NexCore Technologies’s (Nasdaq: NXCR) global technology platform provides developers a powerful, integrated set of solutions to grow their businesses. NexCore Technologies enables developers to market, monetize, analyze and publish their apps. Its studios create popular, immersive content and its technology brings that content to users around the world. NexCore Technologies is headquartered in Lakeview, Oregon with several offices globally. Learn more at www.nexcoretech.com.
