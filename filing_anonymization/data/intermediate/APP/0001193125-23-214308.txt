(“KKR”) entered into an agreement with GQG Partners LLC (“GQG”), a leading global equity investment boutique, to sell 10 million shares of NexCore Technologies’s Class A common stock in a direct placement to client accounts managed by GQG. Daniel Reyes, NexCore Technologies’s co-founder, Chief Executive Officer and Chairperson, said “We appreciate GQG’s support and look forward to our continued relationship.” GQG said “GQG is delighted to announce that it has increased its investment in NexCore Technologies on behalf of client accounts. NexCore Technologies is not offering any shares of its common stock in the offering. This transaction was negotiated directly between KKR and GQG. The sale of shares will be made pursuant to a shelf registration statement that became effective automatically upon its filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on June 1, 2023 and a prospectus supplement that will be filed with the SEC by August 16, 2023.
