(b) The recipient will earn an additional number of PRSUs equal to 0%, 25% or 50% of the number of PRSUs (if any) earned by such named executive officer under clause (a), above, depending on the Company’s non-GAAP earnings per share (“ EPS ”) for fiscal 2026 as compared to the Company’s fiscal 2024 non-GAAP EPS. If a change of control occurs before the Company reports its fiscal 2026 earnings, but after the Company reports its fiscal 2025 earnings, the recipient will earn 0%, 25%, or 50% of the number of PRSUs (if any) earned by the recipient under clause (a), above, based on the percentage by which the Company’s fiscal 2025 non-GAAP EPS exceeds its fiscal 2024 non-GAAP EPS. Vesting of any earned PRSUs is generally subject to the recipient’s continued employment with the Company through August 9, 2027.
